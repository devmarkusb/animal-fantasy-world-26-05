The app (android) should be appealing to 8 +-2 year olds. It should be an animal fantasy world, 3d, free movement, collecting, eating, fighting, hiding. At the beginning the player chooses the animal he wants to be. (Can be an           
'infinite' amount of possibilities, even different kind of horses etc., if possible.)                                                                                                                                              
The world should provide a handful different landscapes/habitats, like ocean, forrest, mountains, sky, ... The chosen animal starts in the world it typically survives in, but is free to move. It should find something to eat    
(and has to, otherwise a progress bar of health decreases) and meet other animals, friend, enemy, prey. The animal should also need to solve 3 tasks to level up. There should be 25 levels. If all levels are won, the   
player can choose to become a fantasy animal, like pegasus, unicorn, mermaide etc. and continue moving through the world being free. State should be saveble to not need to start over again and again.
